Got Questions?

We've got the answers

With self-serve you use our platform to manage your own IP renewals, portfolio management of enforcement. We help you get started and you take it from there.

Our fully managed solutions mean that we take care of everything from document management, to centralized payments and instruction, data management and deadlines.

Whether it's for your renewals or portfolio management or enforcement, you can leave it in our hands.

IP Custodian was founded with the goal of eliminating complexity and increasing transparency in IP management.

Based on the idea that anybody should be able to manage their IP with transparent pricing, choice of foreign counsel and lower costs, making the creation and maintenance of IP accessible for all business sizes.

With Custodian you can optimize counsel fees, improve productivity in day-to-day IP management and achieve this by doing it with your choice of foreign counsel, your service model, focused on your priorities.

You can work with our foreign counsel network which is optimised for quality and costs as well as to simplify document requirements, or you can mix and match, creating your own network.

Our fully managed and self service models work with whichever counsel network you prefer.

Lower costs are achieved through a combination of features.

  1. The IP Custodian foreign counsel network offers negotiated fees and full transparency on pricing to avoid hidden costs such as FX markups, document fees or bank fees.
    We pay renewals in 20+ countries directly to avoid intermediary fees.
  2. Our flat fee service fees mean that you are always clear on what you pay us, no surprises.
  3. You save time. Your data is at your fingertips. Reconciliation is automated, invoicing and document management are centralized. You can even take advantage of our digital signature features for notarization and apostille, in supported jurisdictions.
    This fully integrated solution saves time, hassle and helps keep costs in check. Instead of spending time on invoices, renewals emails and powers, your key staff can use their experience to add value to the business.
