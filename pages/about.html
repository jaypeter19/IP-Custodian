About Custodian

Our Story

We looked at IP management and saw complexity.

Complexity in pricing, administration, and document management. The existing tools often restrict the choice of users, impose long contracts with risky and expensive all-or-nothing implementations. Not viable for small or medium sized IP owners and for larger businesses who could access these tools, they were often dissatisfied with the inflexible legacy technology.

IP Custodian

Transparency

Our Mission is for all IP owners, regardless of size, to have access to a cost-efficient, global foreign counsel, to easily manage their IP portfolios from application to renewals and enforcement and to make it effortless and simple to onboard. We are here to connect IP owners with their foreign counsel using our IP management platform.

As part of this mission, we insisted on incorporating our Values

  • Transparency
  • Flexibility
  • Choice

With our platform you can manage your applications, renewals and enforcement including with integrated watch, so that protecting your IP globally is effortless. If you don't want to manage this yourself, we can do it for you, using the same platform, on a fixed fee basis with no setup costs.

Regardless of the solution you choose, you will enjoy efficient IP management backed by IP professionals and built on an ISO certified platform. Transparent pricing, choice of counsel network and flexibility to change when your business needs it, means that managing your IP globally has never been more straightforward.
